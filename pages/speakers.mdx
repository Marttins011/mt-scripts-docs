# MT Speakers
Advanced speakers/boombox system for QBCore and QBox

**Features**
- Create how many useable speakers items choosing its own prop, music max volume and music max distance
- Save musics to listed whenever you want (saves at SQL)
- On server restart all speakers stay where they've been placed
- Props synced with all players even if player joins server after the placement

**Dependicies**
- qbx_core or qb-core
- ox_target or qb-target
- ox_lib
- xSound
